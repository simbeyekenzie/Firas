Introduction

FIRAS Sunrise Farms grows and delivers farm fresh poultry, vegetables, and dairy. We rise early, harvest at dawn, and handle everything cleanly so families receive nutritious food at its peak.

What began as a small effort has grown into a dependable food partner for homes and businesses. We focus on careful animal welfare, healthy soils, and hygienic handling from farm to pack.

Today we operate across poultry, vegetable, and dairy lines, with fast, same day delivery across our community.

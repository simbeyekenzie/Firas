Who We Are

Rooted in Care, Growing with Purpose

FIRAS Sunrise Farms grows and delivers farm-fresh poultry, vegetables, and dairy. We rise early, harvest at dawn, and handle everything cleanly so families receive nutritious food at its peak.

  • Our StartWhat began as a small effort has grown into a dependable food partner for homes and businesses.
  • Our FocusWe care for animals, nurture healthy soils, and maintain hygienic handling from farm to pack.
  • Our PromiseFast, same-day delivery that keeps your meals fresh and wholesome.

Our Story

From modest beginnings to a multi-line farm operation serving families every day. We still rise at dawn to harvest, collect, sort and deliver at peak freshness.

  1. 2009
    Started small with sunrise harvests and a commitment to quality and care.
  2. 2017
    Expanded operations across poultry and vegetables, improving handling and delivery.
  3. Today
    Added dairy, strengthened hygiene standards, and built a reliable distribution network.
